Welcome to Mine Sweeper

Rules to follow:

Reveal as many boxes as you can

Every revealed box has a count label of possible land mines surrounding it

If you think a box is a land mine, flag it instead of revealing it

If you reveal a land mine, Game Over

You can use the flag and reveal button on the bottom of the screen

You can also left click to reveal and right click to flag
